About Me

Up close and personal

My name is Salik Hassan and I am in my 3rd year of Computer Science Program at York University. My fascination is currently towards Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence because that is going to lead the world in the near future. I am originally from Pakistan which explains my obsession with cricket. I play cricket at a professional level in Canada and aim to break into the national team soon. Archery has also caught my interest and I am taking it up on a weekly basis. My interests and hobbies have shaped me into what I am today. I believe playing cricket is the closest I can come to becoming a warrior. This amazing sport has taught me perseverance, staying focused in high pressure situations, and staring right into the face of adversity when the odds are against me.

Technology has always fascinated me since a very young age but due to financial limitations and lack of modern technology in Pakistan at the time, I could not get to learn much about it. When we moved to Canada in 2014, I got access to the latest technology and I could engross myself in learning about various gadgets till my heart was content. I was gravitated towards back end development due to the sheer possibilities it entailed. I arranged a computer science club in my final year of high school where I, along with my peers,helped junior students get started on their coding journeys with Java. I finally followed up my enthusiasm by getting enrolled in the Computer Science Program at York University. I believe my problem-solving skills and perseverance will aid me to become successful in this program.
